Case Study / Advocacy Membership

AgriFront / Frontline South

6,000 Members

Supporters registered through automated Meta funnels. Verified sign-ups tracked from impression to membership.

The Challenge

Advocacy organizations needed to grow their membership base quickly while maintaining quality and tracking attribution. Manual data entry was creating bottlenecks, and it was impossible to measure which campaigns drove actual sign-ups.

The Solution

Built automated Meta funnels with direct integration into membership databases. Every supporter registration was tracked from initial ad impression through completed membership sign-up, with automated lead nurturing and follow-up flows.

Key Results

  • Automated lead nurturing and follow-up
  • Integration with membership databases
  • 68% reduction in manual data entry
  • 6,000 members with full tracking

The Impact

Organizations now have complete visibility into which campaigns and messaging resonate with potential members. The automated system freed up staff time from manual data entry, allowing them to focus on member engagement and advocacy work.
