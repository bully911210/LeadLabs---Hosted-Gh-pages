Case Study / E-Commerce

E-Commerce Scale

3.2x ROAS

Scaled from R50k to R250k monthly ad spend while maintaining profitability. Revenue tracking from ad to checkout.

The Challenge

A South African fashion e-commerce brand wanted to scale their ad spend but feared losing profitability. They had inconsistent tracking and couldn't determine which products and audiences were actually driving revenue versus just clicks.

The Solution

Implemented complete pixel and Conversion API setup with product-level tracking. Added automated abandoned cart recovery and built custom dashboards showing revenue attribution by campaign, product, and audience.

Key Results

  • Maintained 3.2x ROAS during scale
  • Complete pixel and conversion API setup
  • Automated abandoned cart recovery
  • 5x ad spend increase (R50k → R250k monthly)

The Impact

The brand now scales with confidence, knowing exactly which products, creatives, and audiences drive profitable sales. They've expanded from a single store to multiple locations based on the predictable revenue from Meta campaigns.
